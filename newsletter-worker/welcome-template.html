Hey there 👋,

Thanks for signing up for the hora Calendar waitlist. It means more than you think.

I'm Maciej — I'm building hora solo as an indie developer. It's a native macOS Google Calendar client, built with Swift and SwiftUI. No Electron, no web views, no bloat. Just a fast, calendar that talks directly to Google's API.

The app is already functional — I use it as my daily driver — but there's a lot of work left before it's ready for everyone. I'm shipping updates every day and writing about the entire process publicly: the wins, the bugs, the walls I hit.

As a solo dev, building the app is the easy part. Getting the word out is where I need help. If you know someone who spends their day in Google Calendar — a coworker, a friend, anyone who'd appreciate a faster native experience — I'd be grateful if you shared hora with them.
