Features

3 Calendar Views

Day, Week, and Month views styled after Apple Calendar. Smooth transitions between views.

Full CRUD

Create, edit, delete events with drag and drop. Resize events by dragging edges.

Google Meet

Add conference links directly when creating events. One click to join meetings.

Menu Bar Widget

See today's events and current meeting at a glance from the macOS menu bar.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Google Calendar-style: D/W/M for views, C to create, / to search, J/K to navigate, T for today.

Smart Sync

Incremental sync with configurable intervals. Native macOS notifications for upcoming events.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Hora Calendar is a native macOS application that connects to Google Calendar. Your privacy matters — here is exactly what we access and how we use it.

Data We Access

  • Google Calendar: events and calendars (read & write)
  • Google Other Contacts: names and emails (read only, auto-suggested contacts for guest autocomplete)
  • Google account email address

How We Use It

  • Display and manage your calendar events
  • Provide autocomplete suggestions when adding guests to events
  • Synchronize changes with Google Calendar

Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your Mac using SwiftData
  • No data is sent to any server other than Google APIs
  • No analytics, no tracking, no third-party services

Data Sharing

  • We do not share your data with anyone
  • We do not sell your data

Data Deletion

  • Sign out in Hora Settings to remove all local data
  • Revoke access at myaccount.google.com/permissions
